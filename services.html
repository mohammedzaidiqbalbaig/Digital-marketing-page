Our Services

Overall Checkup

A vehicle health check should cover components such as brakes, oil levels, tire pressure, and other vital engine parts

Engine Tune Up

Engine health is essential to good vehicle performance and lifespan. And one of the best ways to help maintain your engine is by an engine tune-up service

Tire Replacement

Tires should be replaced every 4 years or 40,000 km. Rotating your tires regularly between different positions will ensure that they wear evenly and last longer.

Service 101

Experts recommend checking your car brake fluid, transmission fluids, coolant and windshield washer fluid by removing related covers and visually inspecting each

Change Oil

Most auto manufacturers recommend changing the oil in a vehicle once a year or 20,000 km for petrol engines. For diesel engines and turbo charged petrol engines, the oil should be changed every 6 months or 10,000 km.

Vehicle Spa

A vehicle spa service is a professional treatment that aims to restore and rejuvenate both the exterior and interior of a vehicle. It goes beyond a regular car wash and includes a range of specialized treatments to enhance the overall appearance and condition of the car.
